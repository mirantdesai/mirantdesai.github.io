Philosophy

Humanity and philosophy? How do we transcend intelligence? Is it our comprehension of consciousness and conscientiousness that enables advancement, progress and compassion? A school of thought describes philosophy as the rational, abstract, and methodical consideration of reality as a whole or of fundamental dimensions of human existence and experience. We have tried to answer several elements as it pertains to our understanding of the mind, free will, our pursuit for knowledge, understanding of the self, and the interpretation of god.
