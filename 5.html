The Game of Chess

A game a day keeps the mental qualms away. The game of soccer has many parallels with chess. I tried to acquaint myself to the game, and found much enjoyment in challenging the supercomputer. As you can notice, the AI difficulty is set to the maximum (3200). I played very cagey in the beginning fifteen moves. Once all pieces on the board were proverbial checkmates, it was time for trades. As time progressed, the computer had numeric superiority and covered all possible checks. I was able to navigate about 5 checks towards the end leading up to the checkmate.

Mirant Desai
